About Us!

Her-Digital-Future organisation is a women-focused initiative dedicated to empowering girls and women with digital skills and computer literacy in Africa.
We believe that technology is not just a tool but a gateway to independence, innovation and equality.
Our goal is to bridge the gender gap in technology by providing training, mentorship and opportunities that prepare women for leadership and meaning careers in the digital world. Futhermore, we aim to create a supportive community where every woman, regardless of background, can learn, grow nad thrive in the ever-evolving tech industry.
Her-Digital-Future in partnership with the Google Schorlaships Program 2025 provides 100% FULLY FUNDED ONLINE training for both African girls and women in tech-begginer friendly!
The courses offered are backed by google, so you will be learning from global experts.


Our Mission:

To empower and support women and girls by enabling them to unlock their full potential, participate equally in the digital economy and become changemakers in technology.


Our Vision:

To build a world where women are confident, skilled and equally represented in technology and inspiring the next generation of female tech leaders.
